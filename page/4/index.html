Walkcycle in Perspective

March 27, 2021 路
An exercise in animating a walkcycle on the third Z-dimension. Though animated on 3’s, it’s interesting to see that the further away the character gets, the less the animation seems to be ‘limited’ or jaggy because of the increased overlap in the spacing between the individual frames.
