Walkcycle in Perspective

March 27, 2021
An exercise in animating a walkcycle on the third Z-dimension. Though animated on 3’s, it’s interesting to see that the further away the character gets, the less the animation seems to be ‘limited’ or jaggy because of the increased overlap in the spacing between the individual frames. I suppose one could get away with animating characters or objects in the distance on 3’s. I used the technique from Richard Williams' book on where to place the key positions: read more →
