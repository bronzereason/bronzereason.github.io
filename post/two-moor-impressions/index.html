Jesse Manschot

Two Moor Impressions

January 2, 2022

I went on a long walk through forests and across moors…

… and made some sketches and photos while I was there, adhering to my new year’s resolution to draw more from life and in the world. I decided to expand on two of them by experimenting with watercolor and a layer of black pilot pen and calligraphy brush on top.
