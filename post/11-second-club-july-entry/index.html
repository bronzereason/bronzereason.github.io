11 Second Club: July entry

August 1, 2021

My entry for the 11 Second Club july edition. It is the first time I’m entering this competition and also my first time animating a more rounded (human) character.

I was a bit late to the game, so unfortunately I couldn’t finish the facial animation.

The audio to animate to this month was taken from Atonement (2007). The hard part was coming up with the overall idea; the character in the film recites a letter he wrote, making it unclear where we are diegetically from our point of view. We could be in the same room as him, or in his mind.

Because of this ambiguity I opted for poetic realism. I thought of a soldier in despair of war, seemingly giving up and returning to his memories of promise and joy. There are two contrasting backgrounds; a bleak and dark warzone/beach and a peaceful park.

I tried making the character equally contrasting as well by behaving distinctively in the seperate moments of his life: wading indifferently in the water towards his demise, and ‘swaggering on the promise of life’ towards possibility. The first part is animated on 3’s and the second part on 2’s to underline this.
