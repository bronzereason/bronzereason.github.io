Speed sketching on a Pen Display

August 6, 2021

Recently I acquired a pen display to draw and animate with. I decided to record my timed pose studies to gain an objective view of the way I draw. This is a compilation of the sketches I did of poses ranging between 1-, 2- and 4 minutes.

Krita is the software in which I drew. I’m experimenting with different brushes, pencils and pens between the time ranges. I used OBS Studio to record both the pen display and the secondary screen, on which I used a timed slideshow of models from Proko. Not quite like drawing from a real life model, but it’s as close as I can get!

The music is by Plaid (“Tokyo Drive”).
