The Animation Workshop: Animation Exercises

July 10, 2021

I visited the animation summer school at The Animation Workshop in Viborg, Denmark this year. We got to learn a lot about the fundamentals and principles of animating and drawing. This was also my first (proper) introduction to using TVPaint and a Wacom tablet to animate digitally.

Here are some of the exercises I made to get a grasp on weight, volume, timing, spacing etcetera.
