The Zen of Ice Skating

August 31, 2021 ·

I went back to (mostly) analogue animating for this one. The character is drawn with a calligraphy pen and the background cycle is made of charcoal drawings. All was composited in Opentoonz and digitally colored in Krita.

The ice is a series of five charcoal drawings looping on two layers and blended together:
