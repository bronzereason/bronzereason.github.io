Throwing a Boomerang

May 08, 2021

This time I tried my hand at motion blurring, overlapping action, cutting to a new shot and compositing on backgrounds.

For inspiration I looked at Ohira Shinya’s work.

For preparation I studied a world champion boomerang thrower:
