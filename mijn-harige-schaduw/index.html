Mijn Harige Schaduw en Ik
(My Hairy Shadow and I)

Comedy, Fantasy | 9'50" | NL | 2024

A local TV station visits Karel at his house to feature his wild story about a hair fairy he met online, and the curse it brought into his life. This deadpan mockumentary takes its absurd story very seriously, to hilarious effect.

Premiered at Imagine Fantastic Film Festival, Amsterdam
