An update

2024-05-26

Dear reader,

A couple of updates:

  • I have cleaned this website over the weekend; that means removing some old material, updating the stylesheet and fixing a couple of articles.
  • A week ago I closed my Instagram account. I won't go in too much detail into why, but the gist of it is that it had a negative effect on my creativity and attention.
  • As a consequence, I have made it more convenient to follow me through this website. I will be regularly posting new stuff via monthly updates. These will consist of any new drawings or animations I have added to the website, as well as some previews for new material. You can keep an eye out for the news page, or subscribe to the RSS feed with any of your favourite RSS apps.
